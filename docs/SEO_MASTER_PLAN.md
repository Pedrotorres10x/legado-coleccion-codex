# SEO Master Plan

## Positioning

This project is not a simple property portal.

It is a **360 property buying platform for foreign buyers across Alicante province**, combining:

- property search
- area guidance
- mortgage broker support
- legal coordination
- end-to-end purchase assistance

Every SEO page should reinforce that the user can do more than browse listings. The page should help the buyer:

- understand where to buy
- understand how to buy
- evaluate budget and financing
- trust the legal process
- move toward a supported purchase journey

## Scope

### Main umbrella

- Alicante province

### Important clusters inside the province

- Marina Baixa / norte
- Alicante centro
- Vega Baja / sur

These clusters are useful for internal architecture, internal linking, and content planning.
They are **not** separate strategic umbrellas that should overshadow the province-wide message.

## Golden Rules

### Only create pages for real business coverage

Do not create:

- area guides for places where we do not sell
- generic FAQ pages with no commercial relevance
- speculative SEO pages disconnected from inventory or real service coverage

Create pages only when at least one of these is true:

- we sell there
- we have real inventory or real commercial coverage there
- the page clearly supports the 360 buying journey for an area or intent where we operate

### The website must not sound like a portal

Avoid portal-first framing such as:

- browse listings only
- pure inventory-led copy
- generic local content with no buying process context

Use platform-first framing:

- foreign buyer support
- mortgage guidance
- legal coordination
- end-to-end purchase help

### Financing should be framed strategically

Do not present mortgages only as a necessity.

Content should explain that even buyers who can purchase in cash may still use financing to:

- preserve liquidity
- keep capital available for other investments
- avoid concentrating too much cash in one asset
- retain flexibility after completion

## Core Audience

Foreign buyers, especially from:

- UK
- Netherlands
- Germany
- Belgium
- Scandinavia

Assume the user:

- does not know Spanish property law
- needs clear explanations
- wants practical support, not theory
- is comparing areas, budgets, and purchase structure

## Conversion Principles

Every page should do three things:

1. rank
2. orient
3. convert

Every page should include:

- real inventory or a real route to inventory
- visible 360 support positioning
- mortgage support visibility
- legal coordination visibility
- a clear CTA to speak to the team or start the buying journey

## Tone

Use:

- professional
- clear
- trustworthy
- practical
- premium

Avoid:

- hype
- pushy sales language
- generic tourism framing
- copy that sounds like a commodity portal

## Internal Linking Rules

### Pillar pages should link to:

- municipality pages
- core guides
- top commercial pages

### Zone pages should link to:

- province page
- related commercial pages
- mortgage and legal guides
- sibling areas where comparison makes sense

### Commercial pages should link to:

- parent zone page
- province page
- relevant buying guides
- closely related high-intent pages

## Visual Direction

Pages should feel:

- premium
- clear
- conversion-first
- editorial but commercially sharp

Avoid:

- portal clutter
- excessive filter-heavy layouts
- weak generic headers
- long text walls before inventory

Use:

- strong photography
- expressive but elegant typography
- clear visual hierarchy
- concise trust blocks
- short forms
- obvious CTA hierarchy
